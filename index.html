Kaupapa Ready - Wardrobe Philosophy

Kaupapa Ready helps wahine organise clothing according to five spaces:

  • Tau- calm, grounding, peaceful
  • Kainga- home, comfort, care
  • Ora- energy, vitality, movement
  • Mahi- work, productivity, purpose
  • Kaupapa- events, gatherings, ceremonies

This system encourages intentional dressing and mindful clothing choices.
